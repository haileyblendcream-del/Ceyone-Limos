About Us

Seattle's Trusted Name in Luxury Transportation

At Ceyone Limos, we believe that transportation is more than just getting from point A to point B—it's about the experience. Founded with a commitment to excellence, we have become Seattle's premier choice for luxury limousine services.

Our professional team, led by experienced chauffeurs like Loveish, ensures that every ride is safe, comfortable, and stylish. Whether it's your wedding day, a corporate event, or a night out in the city, we treat every client like a VIP.
